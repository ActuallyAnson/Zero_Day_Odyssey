Player Control 1
W - Move up
A - Move left
S - Move down
D - Move right

Player Control 2
W - Accelerate
S - Decelerate
A - To rotate counter-clockwise
D - To rotate clockwise

Left Click - To shoot
Right Click - To switch between the controls of the player
